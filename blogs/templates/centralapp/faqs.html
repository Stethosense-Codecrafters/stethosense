For Patients

Only verified healthcare professionals registered at Stethosense can access your health record.

No. At Stethosense, we give the privilege to edit the information on your health record only to registered medical professionals whom you choose.

Login to Stethosense and navigate to the "My Doctors" section on your dashboard. You will find an access code on top of the section that opens. This access code is unique to you and is highly confidential. This code shall not be shared with any individual save the doctor whom you wish to grant access.

There is a separate section of your health record which you can edit. This includes information about any medication/supplements that you are taking without medical supervision. You can add the medicines as well as any notes about general well-being that you wish to be displayed to the doctor managing your health records.
