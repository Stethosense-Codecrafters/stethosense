How to use your health record

As a patient, you are given access to only view your medical information keeping the security and authenticity aspects in mind.

Who can make changes to your records ?

Only the doctors approved by and registered at Stethosense can be assigned by you to update your health records.

Start using your health record

As a first time user there might be very little or no information in your medical record. Only the basic vitals collected during the registration would be updated in your profile.
  • Find a doctor already registered on Stethosense and grant permissions to make changes to your record using add a doctor option in your dashboard.
  • View your medication history in the medication section of your dashboard.
  • View your lab reports in the reports section
  • Edit your profile to let the healthcare provider help you better. You can do this under the Edit Profile section.
  • Manage which doctors have access to your profile under the My Doctors section.
  • View the details of operations/surgeries (if any)
